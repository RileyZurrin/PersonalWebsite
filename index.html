Projects

  • A web app for visualizing health and nutritional data from the CDC. The app automatically generates graphs and statistics, providing a fun (and perhaps informative!) experience.

  • A web app that calculates and displays Clebsch-Gordan Coefficients. That is, it converts the angular momenta states of two quantum mechanical systems into those of the combined system (or vice versa). Alternative methods, like using a CG coefficient table, are outdated and clunky.
